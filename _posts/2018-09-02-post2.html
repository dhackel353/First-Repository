--- title: Why Waluigi Should Be In Smash Bros. layout: post ---

For the unacquainted, Nintendo is releasing Smash Bros. Ultimate this December. Fans are generally very satisfied about all the new characters announced and that all old characters are returning.

But there is one character that deserves to be in the game above all others. Waluigi.

Waluigi has been long overdue to being a playable character. Nintendo mocks him by only making him an assist trophy.

He should by in Smash because he is a cool character. He would have an amazing moveset and be beyond a doubt the most played character. His special moves would involve his tennis racquet and bombs.
